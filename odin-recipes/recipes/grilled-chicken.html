Description

Roast chicken, a delicious and popular dish, involves cooking a seasoned chicken until it's tender and flavorful. The crispy, golden-brown skin adds a delightful crunch to the succulent meat.

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken (approximately 3-4 pounds)
  • Olive oil or melted butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh herbs (such as rosemary, thyme, or sage)
  • Garlic cloves (optional)
  • Lemon or other citrus (optional)
  • Vegetables (carrots, onions, potatoes) for roasting alongside the chicken

  6. Tie the chicken's legs together with kitchen twine.
  7. Place the chicken on a roasting pan.

  10. Brush the chicken with its juices every 30 minutes for a golden-brown skin.
  11. Take the chicken out of the oven and let it rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.
