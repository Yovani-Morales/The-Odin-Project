Hot cakes

Description

A hotcake, or pancake, is a tasty breakfast food loved everywhere. It's made from a mix of flour, milk, eggs, and baking powder, cooked until it's golden and fluffy. People often put syrup, butter, fruit, or chocolate chips on top. Hotcakes are a yummy and comforting way to begin the day.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter or oil (for cooking)
  • Optional: vanilla extract, blueberries, chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Mix 1 cup flour, 2 tbsp sugar, 1 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt in a bowl.
  2. Beat 1 egg, add 3/4 cup milk (and a bit of vanilla if you want), and mix in another bowl.
  3. Combine wet and dry mix gently, but don't overmix.
  4. Heat a pan with butter or oil on medium.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup batter for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden.
  7. Repeat and add more butter or oil as needed.
  8. Serve warm with syrup, butter, fruit, or chocolate chips on top.
  9. Enjoy your homemade pancakes!
