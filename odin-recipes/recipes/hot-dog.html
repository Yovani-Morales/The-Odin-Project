Description

A hot dog is a popular snack that includes a cooked sausage inside a bun. People usually add toppings like ketchup, mustard, onions, and more. It's a simple and quick meal often enjoyed at outdoor events and picnics.

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Cooked sausage
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Chopped onions
  • Pickles (relish)
  • Other optional sauces and condiments

Instructions

  1. Cook the sausages until they're hot and browned.
  2. Put the sausages in the buns.
  3. Add ketchup and mustard.
  4. Sprinkle onions on top.
  5. Put some pickle relish if you like.
  6. You can add other sauces too.
  7. Serve the hot dogs while they're warm.
  8. Enjoy your tasty hot dogs!
